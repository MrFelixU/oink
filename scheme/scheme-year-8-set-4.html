n1

Calculations and rounding

Textbook: [N1.1][N1.2][N1.3]

Online: [N1.1][N1.2][N1.3]

  • Use written methods to divide a large number by a single digit
  • Use written methods to complete the four operations with decimals
  • Order a set of numbers
  • Add and subtract negative numbers
  • Multiply and divide negative numbers
  • Find the square and square root of a number
  • Find the cube and cube root of a number
  • Multiply and divide numbers by powers of 10
  • Multiply and divide numbers by negative powers of 10
  • Round numbers to the nearest whole, 10 or 100
  • Round to a number of decimal places
  • Estimate calculations by rounding
 Positive, Negative, Difference, Inverse, Divisible, Multiple, Lowest common multiple (LCM), Factor, Common factor, Highest common factor (HCF), Prime number, Square, Square root (), Inverse, Power, Cubed

Online: [A1.1][A1.2]

  • Describe the basic rules of algebra, including a x a = a2, ab means a x b and 7a means 7 lots of a
  • Understand that a x a x a x a x a = a5 and can use index notation to simplify algebra
  • Use BIDMAS to complete a calculation
  • Describe what is meant by a like term
  • Simplify algebra by collecting like terms
  • Expand a single bracket
  • Expand two sets of single brackets and simplify
  • Write an expression to describe a simple scenario
 Sequence, Term, Position, Term-to-term rule, Increasing sequence, Decreasing sequence, Function machine, Input, Output, Function, Sequence, Sequence table, Position-to-term rule

gm1

Angles and constructions

Textbook: [GM1.1][GM1.2][GM1.3]

Online: [GM1.1][GM1.2][GM1.3]

  • Identify 3D and 2D shapes by name and count their vertices, faces and edges
  • Identify types of triangle and quadrilateral and describe their properties
  • Draw and measure angles
  • Calculate missing angles on straight lines and around a point
  • Calculate missing angles in triangles and quadrilaterals
  • Identify corresponding, alternate and co-interior/supplementary angles in parallel lines
  • Solve problems involving simple angle properties and angles in parallel lines
  • Use a compass and protractor
  • Perform simple constructions using the correct equipment (perp bisector, angle bisector, constructing triangles)
 Angles around a point, Angles on a straight line, Vertically opposite angles, Reflex angle, Vertex, vertices, Construction, Construct, Construction lines, Parallel, Perpendicular, Vertex, vertices, Quadrilateral, Rectangle, Square, Parallelogram, Rhombus, Trapezium, Isosceles trapezium, Kite, Arrowhead, Coordinates, Alternate angles, Corresponding angles
