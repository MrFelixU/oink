Calculations, rounding and standard form

Textbook: [N1.1][N1.2][N1.3]

Online: [N1.1][N1.2][N1.3]

  • Use written methods to divide a large number by a single digit
  • Use written methods to complete the four operations with decimals
  • Complete the four operations with negative numbers
  • Multiply and divide numbers by powers of 10
  • Multiply and divide numbers by negative powers of 10
  • Write numbers in standard form
  • Change numbers from standard form back into ordinary numbers
  • Round to a number of decimal places
  • Round to a number of significant figures
  • Estimate calculations by rounding
  • Recognise reciprocals
 Powers of 10, Index, indices, Negative index, Standard form, Inverse operation, Reciprocal, Round, Decimal place, Significant figure, Estimate
