Year 7 Set 4

Autumn First Half Term (HT1) (8 weeks)

Teaching Assessment

Types of Numbers

  • Use written methods to multiply numbers with up to 3 digits
  • Use written methods to divide a large number by a single digit
  • Add and subtract negative numbers
  • Multiply and divide negative numbers
  • Order decimals from smallest to largest
  • Complete the four operations with decimals
  • Multiply and divide by powers of 10
  • Describe what is meant by the terms factor and multiple
  • List factors and multiples of a number
  • Find the HCF and LCM of two or more numbers
  • Describe what is meant by a prime number
  • Find the square and square root of a number
  • Find the cube and cube root of a number

Length, area and perimeter

  • Use a ruler to accurately measure or draw a line
  • Calculate the area of a rectangle and state the units
  • Calculate the area of a triangle, and parallelogram
  • Calculate the perimeter of a rectangle and state the units
  • Find the length of missing sides in compound shapes
  • Calculate the area and perimeter of a compound shape
  • Convert between metric measurements

Algebra

  • Describe the basic rules of algebra, including a x a = a2, ab means a x b and 7a means 7 lots of a
  • Understand that a x a x a x a x a = a5 and can use index notation to simplify algebra
  • Describe what is meant by a like term
  • Simplify algebra by collecting like terms
  • Substitute values into an expression
  • Expand a single bracket
  • Apply the order of operations to a calculation correctly
  • Write an expression to describe a simple scenario

Year 7 Set 4

Autumn Second Half Term (HT2) (7 weeks)

Teaching Assessment

Fractions and percentages

  • Understand that the line in a fraction means divide
  • Recall simple FDP conversions e.g. 0.5, 0.75, 0.25, 0.1
  • Convert between fractions, decimals and percentages
  • Find equivalent fractions
  • Write a set of fractions in order of size
  • Add and subtract fractions
  • Calculate a percentage of an amount without a calculator
  • Calculate percentage increase and decrease
  • Find a fraction of an amount

Averages and probability

  • Find the mean, median, mode and range of a set of data
  • Solve problems with averages
  • Understand that a probability can be written as a fraction, decimal or percentage
  • Calculate the probability of an event when outcomes are equally likely
  • Find the probability that an event does not occur
  • Use the fact that probability adds up to a whole to solve problems

Sequences

  • Complete a mapping diagram
  • Find the input of a mapping diagram when given the output
  • Identify the term to term rule of a sequence
  • Continue a sequence using the term to term rule
  • Work out the nth term rule of a sequence
  • Use the nth term rule to calculate any term of a sequence
  • Solve problems involving sequences

Year 7 Set 4

Spring First Half Term (HT3) (6 weeks)

Teaching Assessment

Angles and shapes

  • Name different 2D and 3D shapes and list their properties
  • Name different types of angles and list their properties
  • Use a protractor to draw and measure angles
  • Calculate missing angles on straight lines and around a point
  • Calculate missing angles in triangles and quadrilaterals
  • Name different triangles and quadrilaterals and list their properties
  • Recognise lines of symmetry in shapes
  • Identify the order of rotational symmetry of a shape

Using a calculator and rounding

  • Use a calculator effectively
  • Use the SD button
  • Use the ANS button
  • Use square root, square, cube, powers, fractions on the calculator
  • Round to the nearest 10, 100, 1000
  • Round to a number of decimal places
  • Estimate the answer to a calculation by rounding

Ratio and proportion

  • Simplify a ratio
  • Simplify a ratio that involves different units
  • Split a quantity into a ratio
  • Solve problems involving ratios
  • Understand what is meant by direct proportion
  • Solve simple direct proportion problems
  • Solve problems using the unitary method
  • Solve problems involving recipes

Teaching Assessment

Solving equations

  • Find missing values in simple worded problems such as I start with a number
  • Solve simple linear equations including ones with answers that are decimals or negatives or need to be left as fractions

Data representation

  • Write an appropriate question for a survey
  • Understand what sampling is and when it can be biased
  • Draw and interpret pictograms
  • Draw and interpret bar charts
  • Draw a frequency table for grouped and ungrouped data
  • Draw and interpret pie charts
  • Draw and interpret scatter graphs

Linear graphs

  • Substitute values into a formula
  • Plot coordinates in all four quadrants
  • Fill in a table of values
  • List coordinates from a table of values
  • Draw a linear graph
  • Understand what the word linear means
  • Draw the graphs of horizontal and vertical lines and the graph of y=x
  • Read values from a graph

Summer First Half Term (HT5) (5 weeks)

Teaching Assessment

Transformations

  • Reflect a shape in an axis
  • Reflect a shape in any line
  • Rotate a shape around the origin
  • Rotate a shape around any point
  • Translate a shape by a description
  • Translate a shape by a vector
  • Perform combinations of rotation, reflection and translation
  • Describe a single transformation
  • Identify when shapes are congruent

Volume and surface area

  • Draw plans and elevations of 3D shapes
  • Identify nets of cuboids
  • Describe the difference between volume and surface area
  • Calculate the volume and surface area of a cube and cuboid
  • Convert between metric measurements
  • Solve problems involving area, volume and surface area

Measures and constructions

  • Read and interpret different scales
  • Convert between different metric units
  • Identify the number of edges, faces and vertices in a 3D shape
  • Draw a net for a solid shape
  • Identify types of triangles and know their properties
  • Use a compass and protractor
  • Construct triangles using the correct equipment
